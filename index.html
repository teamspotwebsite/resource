TeamSpot

An Android and iOS app to allows you to find team or teammates for project-based classes at Georgia Tech.

Get Started

TeamSpot is a mobile app where students can share project ideas, find teams, join teams, and meet new friends within the same class. Our goal is to help students form teams for group project-based classes easier.
